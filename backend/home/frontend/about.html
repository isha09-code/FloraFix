About Florafix

Florafix is an AI-powered platform designed to help farmers and gardeners identify plant diseases quickly and accurately while promoting sustainable farming.

🌿 Our Mission

  • ✔️ Reduce crop losses due to undiagnosed or misdiagnosed plant diseases
  • ✔️ Promote sustainable farming and gardening practices
  • ✔️ Minimize unnecessary pesticide use through precise diagnostics
  • ✔️ Educate users about plant diseases and their prevention

⚙️ The Technology

  • ✔️ Detects 39+ different plant diseases and healthy plants
  • ✔️ Supports 14+ different plant species including crops and garden plants
  • ✔️ Recognizes symptoms with high accuracy using AI
  • ✔️ Built on advanced deep learning and image recognition models
